About CollegeFinder

India's smartest college recommendation platform — built for students, by students.

Our Mission

CollegeFinder was built to solve one of India's biggest student challenges — finding the right college. With hundreds of engineering colleges across the country, and limited guidance, students often make uninformed decisions.

We use a smart recommendation engine that considers your academic scores, entrance rank, budget, category, and preferences to match you with colleges where you'll genuinely thrive.

Built With Modern Tech

A full-stack MERN application with industry-standard practices

⚛️

Frontend

HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript with glassmorphism UI, responsive design, and Chart.js analytics.

🟢

Backend

Node.js + Express.js REST APIs with JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, and CORS support.

🍃

Database

MongoDB with Mongoose ODM — stores colleges, users, saved lists, reviews, and analytics data.
